Carlota Mojica

Graphic and visual designer based in Málaga (Spain). My multidisciplinary work focuses mainly on art direction, brand identity development, editorial design and web design for artists, companies, organizations, collectives and individuals. Alongside my client work, I also develop personal projects, driven by a continuous interest in visual experimentation.

I’m currently part of the team at bRIDA, a brand strategy and design studio based in Málaga.

I also occasionally take on freelance commissions that allow for a more personal and exploratory approach to design.

For collaborations or inquiries, feel free to reach out at info@carlotamojica.work
